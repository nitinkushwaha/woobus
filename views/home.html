Main Features

Woobus plans on solving these problems providing a standardised bus service across India. We will achieve this goal by tying up with bus operators under the Woobus brand and ensuring services in their buses. We aim at creating a national brand with which consumers have an assurance of a high quality service. The features/services we plan on providing in the buses are

LEG SPACE

Buses will be customised to provide sufficient leg space to each traveler.

CACHEFI WIFI

The CacheFi wifi is a platform built to provide reliable content to the users irrespective of the the network conditions.

ACCESSORIES

Eye masks, blankets, ear phones, ear buds, puke bags etc.

CHARGING

Power sockets for each traveler
